element	intro
	This tab provides visualizations on gene expression changes.
visDEG	Click here to explore different visualizations on the DEGs. <b>Volcano</b> and <b>Heatmap</b> plots display the top/significant changing genes. <b>Explore a gene</b> allows you to zoom into the trend of a single gene via violin and box plots. 
ui_volcano	Here's the volcano plot that highlights the top DEGs.
vplot_parameters	Parameters to generate the volcano plot can be adjusted here.
volcano_confirm	Click <b>Visualize</b> to re-draw the volcano plot.
v_download	Click to download the volcano plot for your record.
